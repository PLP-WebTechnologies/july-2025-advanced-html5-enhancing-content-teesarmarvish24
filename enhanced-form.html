Emergency Services

Call the appropriate service below for immediate assistance before filing a report:

  1. North America (USA/Canada): Ambulance/Police/Fire 911
  2. Europe (Germany/France): Ambulance/Police/Fire 112
  3. Africa (Nigeria): Ambulance/Police/Fire 112
  4. Africa (South Africa): Police 10111, Ambulance 10177
  5. Africa (Kenya): All services 999
  6. Africa (Egypt): Police 122, Ambulance 123, Fire 180
  7. Africa (Morocco): Ambulance/Fire 15, Police 19
  8. South America (Brazil): Police 190, Ambulance 192, Fire 193
  9. South America (Argentina): All services 911
